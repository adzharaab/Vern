EDUCATIONAL JOURNEY

📚 My Experiences

This school year was full of new lessons, activities, projects, and friendships, and conflicts I sometimes had to face on my own.

💪 Challenges I Faced

I can admit that it was a big change for me as I transferred here, specially with its change of environment and with every other people I meet, I faced difficulty in fitting in, and wanting to belong, besides the actual activities like, performance tasks, unexpected deadlines, I somehow managed to finish, was just someone who's used to figuring out everything on their own.

🏆 Achievements I Am Proud Of

I am proud of completing my requirements and improving myself for me, and for others around me.

✨ Lessons I Learned

I learned that hard work, patience, and determination are important in achieving your goals. I learned that every conflict, every difficulty always works out in the end, and it delivers amazing lessons for us even if it was hard, and also sometimes a reminder that God is always good.
